about us
Eight hotel brands. Limitless possibilities.
Minor Hotels is a global hospitality leader with more than 550 hotels and resorts across six continents

With decades of expertise, Minor Hotels connects guests to the world's most vibrant destinations. From tropical resorts to urban retreats, we craft experiences that celebrate local culture, thoughtful design, and world-class service. Whether for adventure, relaxation, or business, our hotels ensure every stay is memorable and meaningful.

Operating in over 60 countries with a dedicated team of 40,000, Minor Hotels combines global reach with local charm. We invite guests to discover unforgettable journeys, from bustling cities to serene natural escapes. With every stay, Minor Hotels creates opportunities for connection, discovery, and lasting memories.
